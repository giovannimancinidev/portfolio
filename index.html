    Cinder City

    •

    • Personal Project

    • itch.io

    • C#

    • 2021

     

    Cinder City is a hand-drawn point-and-click adventure about a society forced to live in an underground city for safety and necessity, and a little kid whose curiosity will make him discover long-held secrets. Story It’s been over two hundred years since Phoenix Corp. built Cinder City, an underground metropolis where humankind now lives. Phoenix Corp. controls everything in the city, being the major supplier of SHINE, the most important resource to keep it all running- the city as much as the lives that inhabit it. Everything is made out to be perfect by the corporation, but the truth above everyone’s heads is anything but. No one knows anything about the surface nowadays and, save from the tales your grandfather used to put you to sleep with, you are no exception. Stories about stars, sunlight and oceans, animals you are not sure if he made up or not. All you know is that, for as long as you can remember, your dream was to see it all, and there was only one place where you could start to seek answers: Phoenix Corp.'s headquarters. Mechanics and Features Move and interact with the world through a click of the mouse Interact with the scene to pick up items and trigger animations Solve puzzles to unlock your way Craft new configurations for the Omnikey Collectables you find expand on the story Use stealth or tricks to avoid guards Plenty of easter eggs to discover (can you catch find them all?) Game Controls Interacting Use the Left Mouse Button to interact with the world around you -- be sure to explore, there are many surprises awaiting! Using objects Sometimes you'll need specific items to unlock your way, equip objects in the Inventory tab with the Right Mouse Button. Crafting Drag items from the Inventory slots to the left Crafting slots to craft new Omnikey configurations. To disassemble them, drag the simple items back to the Inventory slots.

    Rino's Diner Crime

    •

    • Personal Project

    • Blueprints

    • 2022

     

    Sant'Andrea, 1980, Don Romero is released from prison after serving 30 years in prison and the first thing he decides to do is enjoy his favorite dish at his trusted diner. When he arrives at the place, however, his desire is momentarily cut short by the inexorable passage of time that has led his beloved diner to be a disused and dilapidated place. Stubborn in his desire to appease his desire, he orders his two henchmen to find anyone who may still be able to replicate the recipe or, in case of failure, executed them both. During their research they discover that the only surviving “cook” for the recipe is the grandson of the original inventor of the dish and owner of the now defunct diner. Once captured, the boy is forced to prepare the dish in the abandoned diner under threat of death. Ciruzzo's only hope is to stall until the police arrive. Game: Choice of recipe based on ingredients Quick events to prepare each ingredient Different paths of combinations in order to use as much time as possible for the preparation of the entire recipe Based on the time taken at the end there are 3 possible endings: death, unknown, rescue by the cops

    The Unknown Project

    •

    • Thesis Project

    • Blueprints

    • 2022

     

    The Unknown Project is an action adventure game developed in Unreal Engine 5, exploratory and characterized by various environmental puzzles. The project was born as an academic thesis and aims to explore the theme of pathologies in video games, the bonds that are established and the possibility in them of bringing to light new creative frontiers on the design side while at the same time giving a voice to all people who would like to make their illness known more. Story concept: Ashlee finds herself in a place that is both familiar and anonymous and disorienting at the same time. A ladder can allow her to escape but only if made accessible, not a simple act given that even the simplest of steps can make the tracks she just followed disappear. What do these walls hide? Will it be possible to orient yourself?

    Great Kyle Escape

    •

    • Personal Project

    • C#

    • 2022

     

    Great Kyle Escape is an infinite runner and sound reactive game, in which the player is called to finish the path in front of the robot protagonist, dodging every object that hinders him thanks to an impressive agility and to the commands of the Arduino controller: the gyroscope and the potentiometer of the board allow you to move Kyle to the right and to the left with only the inclination of the controller in the respective directions; while the present microphone allows, by recording the volume variations, to make Kyle pirouette in a pike leap by means of sonorous incitements. Furthermore, the obstacles within each level are generated randomly in time with the song chosen for the level: in this there is a typical rythm game mechanics for which the bpm of the song affects the difficulty of the level, the more bpm there is greater will be the number of obstacles produced and their scanning over time.

    The Only Way Is Us

    •

    • Personal Project

    • itch.io

    • C#

    • 2021

     

    The Only Way Is Us is a 2D local co-op adventure game based on the greek myth of Daedalus and Icarus. Working together, the players will help each other to try and escape the Labyrinth in which the characters are trapped. Story On King Minos' orders, greek inventor Daedalus built the Labyrinth, a complex prison created to contain the Minotaur. Once Daedalus ultimated the construction, since he was the only one to know the complete layout of the Labyrinth, King Minos decided to imprison the inventor and his son, Icarus, inside his own creation. In order to escape, Daedalus is now trying to build large wings out of bird feathers and wax. But even for a genius like him it may take some trial and error before figuring out what works best... Mechanics and Features Play together using the same keyboard (wasd + directional arrows) collaborate to solve environmental puzzles About the project A game-jam-like exam in which we had to follow a theme and respect the chosen diversifiers during the whole development process.
